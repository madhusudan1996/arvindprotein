The Beginning of Our Journey

25 years of expertise.

We are Manufacturer, Processor & Exporter of Oilseeds, Pulses & Spices from the Saurashtra region of Gujarat-India. Our company was established in 1998 with the vision to provide quality agricultural products right from the farmers.

We have a great team & network for procuring quality raw materials that are required by international processors & traders. We fulfill the quality expectations of our buyers while maintaining hygienic standards.

We have our own modern automated processing plant, with a capacity of 100 M.T./Day. Our state-of-the-art IEC 22000-2018 certified processing plant ensures cleanliness, sorting, and grading without human exposure.
